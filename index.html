How to play

You have awakened in a strange place, and you must fight through 5 different areas to make it out alive! You have 60 seconds in each area to defeat as many enemies as you can! But be careful, you only have 3 lives, and the enemies become stronger with each area you make it to.

  • W: Move Up
  • S: Move Down
  • A: Move Left
  • D: Move Right
  • Spacebar: Attack
